Frequently Asked Questions

What are the store hours?

Most food stores have specific opening and closing times, which can vary by location. It's best to check the store's website or call ahead to confirm their hours of operation.

Do you offer online shopping or delivery services?

Many food stores now provide online shopping options and home delivery services. You can usually find this information on their website or through their mobile app.

What is your return policy for groceries?

Food stores typically have a return policy for perishable and non-perishable items. It's advisable to keep your receipt and check the store's policy regarding returns and exchanges.

Can I find locally sourced products?

Many food stores prioritize local sourcing and may have a section dedicated to local products. Inquire with staff about the availability of locally sourced items.
